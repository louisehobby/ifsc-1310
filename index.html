Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is a strategy that web designers use to emphasize content more than the presentation and clinet slide scripting. It's useful because it allows webdesigners to focus on developing the best version of their website, while mainly focusing on the content section. It's a philosophy in which content is emphasized and additional features are layered. Progressive enhancement is a strategy in web design that puts emphasis on web content first.
